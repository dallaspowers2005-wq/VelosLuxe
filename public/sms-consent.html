SMS Communication Consent

VelosLuxe provides AI-powered communication services on behalf of our medical spa partners. When you interact with one of our partner med spas, you may receive automated SMS messages for appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and client reactivation campaigns.

Provide Your Consent

How Users Opt In

Consent is collected through the following methods before any SMS messages are sent:

  • 1 Clients provide their phone number when booking appointments via the med spa's website or at the front desk, where SMS consent language is clearly displayed.
  • 2 Clients check a consent checkbox on digital intake forms and online booking forms before submission.
  • 3 Verbal consent is obtained and recorded during phone calls with the med spa's staff or AI receptionist.
  • 4 All contacts can reply STOP at any time to immediately unsubscribe from all future SMS messages.

Message Types

Appointment confirmations, appointment reminders (48hr, 24hr, 1hr), post-visit follow-ups, review requests, and reactivation messages for inactive clients.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on appointment activity. Typically 2–6 messages per appointment cycle. No more than 10 messages per month.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive a one-time confirmation and no further messages.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at support@velosluxe.com.
